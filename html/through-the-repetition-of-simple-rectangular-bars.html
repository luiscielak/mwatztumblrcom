Through the repetition of simple rectangular bars...

November 06, 2011
Through the repetition of simple rectangular bars that cross over at different converging points, Morellet creates an optical play that engages the spectator as they attempt to focus on the intricately arranged composition but they are forced to give up as the pattern gently overpowers the eye.
 
Stefanie Kogler, exhibition review: ‘Abstraction-Creation- Post-War Geometric Abstract Art from Europe and South America’, Austin Desmond Fine Art, Bloomsbury, London, 8th September – 6th October 2010
