Brilliant honesty. Being an artist does not make...

June 14, 2012

Brilliant honesty. Being an artist does not make you exempt from feeling fat and unwanted, kids. Be prepared.

Q: Not long after you and your lover and collaborator, Ulay, broke up in 1988, you got a breast enlargement, which some found to be anathema to the feminist tradition of performance art.

A: I don’t care. You know, I was 40 years old. I heard that Ulay made pregnant his 25-year-old translator. I was desperate. I felt fat, ugly and unwanted, and this made a huge difference in my life. Why not use technology if you can, if it can build your spirit? And I’m not feminist, by the way. I am just an artist.

 
The Devil in Marina Abramovic - NYTimes.com (via Kyle Chayka)
